about me
A creative individual and proud mom of two children and a Siberian Husky puppy. Painting has been my way of expressing myself ever since I was a young child; the paintbrush has always been my connection from reality into an alternate reality where I can freely express myself and feel blissful, disconnected from all external distractions and problems. Every painting touched by the paintbrush in my hand is detailed until is reaches my personal level of perfection. I hope that other people find my work as detailed and breathtaking as my family and I do.
